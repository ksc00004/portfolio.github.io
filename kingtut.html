KING TUT MUSEUM PACKAGE

An assorted package to be sold at the gift shop of the Immersive King Tut experience. Included is a board game, book, and welcome note.

This project required me to stay organized with all of my content and files as well as construct an overarching theme that lifted the content. The book is 84 pages long, and I've selected a few spreads to share.
