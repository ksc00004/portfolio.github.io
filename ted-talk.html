TED TALK MAGAZINE SPREAD

This project used the design process to a T. It taught me the importance of idea generation. I created an image tailored to the message of a ted-talk to live within a magazine spread.

THUMBNAILS

Idea generation allows you to filter through easy thoughts so that you can create unique and well-developed ideas. A project without thoughtful ideas is very limited in its ability to grow. To get this going promptly I sketch a bunch of small sloopy ideas.

REFINED SKETCHES

I then take a handful of my best ideas and draw them out larger and more detailed.

DIGITIAL ROUGHS

I then take those detailed sketches and quickly render them digitally to get an idea of which one would do the best with more time.
